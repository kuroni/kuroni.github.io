Hi! I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by [Prof. Shuchi Chawla](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/~shuchi/). Before joining UT, I was as an undergraduate research assistant at Purdue University, working with [Prof. Alex Psomas](https://www.alexpsomas.com/) and [Prof. Paul Valiant](https://www.cs.purdue.edu/homes/pvaliant/).

My primary research interest lies at the intersection of computer science and economics. As of now, I am primarily focusing on optimal stopping theory, mechanism design, and fair allocation. I am also interested in theoretical computer science in general, especially algorithmic statistics, machine learning theory, and combinatorial algorithms.

I also have many interests apart from research, including competitive programming and rhythm games (see more [here](/others/)).
